Our Story

Capturing Moments

Welcome to Artmine Photography — Baringo County's premier photography studio, nestled in the heart of Kabarnet. We are passionate visual storytellers dedicated to immortalising your most cherished moments with artistry, precision, and heart.

Led by our visionary CEO, Artmine Photography has grown to become the most trusted name across the Rift Valley region — from grand weddings to corporate events, aerial photography to intimate portraits. We don't just take photos; we craft legacies.
